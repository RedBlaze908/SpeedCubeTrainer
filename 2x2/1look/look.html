1-Look

2x2

One Looking is one of the most important techniques for solving the 2x2 cube in very few seconds.

The Technique consists of predicting in inspection all the moves you are going to make to solve the cube, so you predict how to do the first layer (CLL, EG1, EG2) and then try to predict which case you will get in the top layer.

This technique can be difficult at first but with time it will get easier and easier, to start with you learn basic cases where you insert only one piece to then more pieces to insert, here on this page I try to show how to create a first layer in a simple and efficient way.
