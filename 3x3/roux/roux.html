Roux

Roux is one of the most used methods to solve the cube, and it is also the method that Use fewer moves ever to solve it (average move count is 48 moves).
The method is divided into four steps: FB, SB, CMLL, LSE or L6E
Roux is also considered as one of the best for 3x3 oh.
